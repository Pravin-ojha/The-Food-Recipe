Biryani Recipe Details

Ingredients Required:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 200g chicken
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Time Required:

Preparation Time: 20 minutes
Cooking Time: 40 minutes

Steps to Cook:

  1. Soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes, then cook until 70% done. Set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil/ghee and sauté the sliced onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, and biryani masala. Cook until the tomatoes are soft.
  4. Add chicken and cook until fully done. Add yogurt and mix well.
  5. In a separate pot, layer the chicken mix and partially cooked rice. Garnish with coriander and mint leaves.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes until the flavors are absorbed.
  7. Serve hot with raita.

Chana Masala Recipe

Ingredients Required:

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp chana masala powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Time Required:

Prep Time: 8 hours (for soaking chickpeas) | Cook Time: 40 minutes

Steps to Cook:

  1. Boil the soaked chickpeas in a pressure cooker with some salt until soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for another minute.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil starts separating from the masala.
  5. Add turmeric, coriander powder, red chili powder, and chana masala powder. Stir well.
  6. Add the boiled chickpeas and mix well with the masala.
  7. Add water as needed to get the desired consistency and let it simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.

Halwa Recipe

Ingredients Requirement:

  • 1 cup Semolina (Suji)
  • 1/2 cup Ghee
  • 1/2 cup Sugar
  • 2 cups Water
  • Cardamom (optional)
  • Nuts for garnish (optional)

Time Required:

Total Time: 30 minutes

Steps to Cook:

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and add semolina (suji). Roast it until it turns golden brown.
  2. In another pan, heat water and sugar until the sugar dissolves completely.
  3. Slowly pour the sugar syrup into the roasted semolina and stir continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook until the halwa thickens and leaves the sides of the pan. Add cardamom and garnish with nuts.
  5. Serve hot.

Maggi Recipe

Ingredients Required:

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 1/4 tsp salt (optional)
  • 1 tsp oil or butter
  • Vegetables (optional - carrots, peas, onions)

Time Required:

Approx. 10 minutes

Steps to Cook:

  1. Boil 1.5 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add the Maggi noodle cake and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the Maggi tastemaker and stir well.
  4. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the water is absorbed.
  5. Optional: Add vegetables, oil, or butter for extra flavor.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Time Required:

Preparation: 10 minutes
Cooking: 20 minutes

Steps to Cook:

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté the onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Stir in the tomato puree, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt.
  4. Let it cook for 5-7 minutes until the oil separates.
  5. Add cream and mix well.
  6. Gently add the paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot.

Aloo Tikki Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 4 boiled potatoes
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • 1 finely chopped green chili
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for shallow frying

Time Required:

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Steps to Cook:

  1. In a bowl, mash the boiled potatoes.
  2. Add breadcrumbs, green chili, cumin powder, garam masala, and salt.
  3. Mix well and shape into small patties.
  4. Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry the patties until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Serve hot with chutney or sauce.

Cake Recipe

Time Required: 1 hour 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup milk

Steps to Cook:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix the flour and baking powder together.
  3. Cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Add the eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture and milk, mixing until smooth.
  6. Stir in the vanilla extract.
  7. Pour the batter into a greased cake tin.
  8. Bake for 45-50 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Let the cake cool, then serve and enjoy!
